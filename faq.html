Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need? Contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Account & Verification
Do I need to verify my identity?

Yes — KYC verification is required before you can withdraw funds. It's a one-time step you complete from your dashboard, and our compliance team reviews submissions manually.

How long does KYC review take?

Submissions are reviewed manually by our team. You'll see your status update on your dashboard once it's been reviewed.

What if my KYC submission is rejected?

You can resubmit directly from your dashboard with corrected documents.

Deposits
What's the minimum deposit?

You can fund your wallet with as little as ₦500 via bank transfer.

How do I deposit funds?

From your dashboard, go to Deposit, transfer the amount to the bank details shown, then enter your transfer sender details so our team can match and confirm it.

How long until my deposit is confirmed?

Deposits are reviewed and approved manually by our team. Your wallet is credited as soon as your transfer is confirmed.

Withdrawals
How do I withdraw my funds?

Request a withdrawal from your dashboard with your bank account details. Our team reviews and pays out requests manually, usually within 24 hours.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Yes, the minimum withdrawal request is ₦1,000.

What happens if my withdrawal request is rejected?

If a request is rejected, the amount was never deducted from your wallet in the first place, so there's nothing to refund — your balance simply remains as it was.

Investments & Returns
How are my funds protected?

Your capital is spread across diversified, real-world backed products rather than a single asset, reducing exposure to any one sector's downturn.

How often are returns paid?

Daily returns post to your wallet every day your investment is active — visible in real time on your dashboard.

Can I invest in more than one product at a time?

Yes, you can hold multiple active investments across different products simultaneously.

VIP Program
How do I move up a VIP tier?

Tiers are based on your total deposits, number of active investments, and referrals. Higher tiers unlock a bigger daily bonus.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help before you make your first deposit.
